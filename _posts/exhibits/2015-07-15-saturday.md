Following on from my press-up series from Thursday morning, I set the self-timer on my camera for 2 seconds and ran as fast as I could away from the camera. I would then run back to the camera once I had been photographed and initiated the 2 second countdown again. 

In total, there are 33 photographs of me doing these 2 second interval runs. By the end I became too tired to continue.
