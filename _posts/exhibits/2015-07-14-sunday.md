_2 second undress_

Undressing (with all of my coats on) in the space of 2 seconds via the self-timer option on my camera on a cold night.

I stopped when I was no longer cold from doing this exercise.

_"It is better to be an artwork than an artist_" - Friedrich Nietzsche
